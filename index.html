ABOUT VCW

The Vivekanandha College for Women has embarked on a journey in pursuit of excellence. It was established in the year 2005 in the name of the great Saint Vivekanandha. The college is situated on either side of Tiruchengode and Namakkal Main Road at Elayampalayam, and 6 kms away from Tiruchengode. Of late, the institution has been illuminated by the rare effulgence and radiance of His Excellency, Former President of India, Dr. A.P.J. Abdulkalam’s visit as a Guest Speaker.His address inspired the institution in pursuing the future course of action.

ABOUT CS DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science and Computer Applications was established in 2005. Our mission is to practice young women in the field of computer to acquire many opportunities and ensure that they have a solid foundation in the core concepts, equip them with problem solving and decision making skills and prepare them for lifelong learning in the discipline. Department also encourages collegial, intellectual and academic growth of its faculty. The department offers Under Graduate, Post Graduate Courses and the Research Programme in Master of Philosophy.

PLACEMENT

The placement and training cell has been established at the institute for providing necessary knowledge skills apart from the curriculum to face the campus recruitment requirements and to train students and raise employability keeping in mind their career ambition. The placement cell also develops interaction regularly with the leading IT and other science laboratories for the students to understand the potential skill and knowledge requirement in the industries and for their development and placement in the industries.

placement cell activities

  • Resume Preparation
